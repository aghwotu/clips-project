Clipz Project

An app where you can upload your gaming videos and share them with everyone.

Features:

  • Login & Logout
  • Upload your gaming video clips
  • Generate screenshots from your uploaded video
  • Manage your all your videos
  • Organize your videos by: 'Recent Uploads' or 'Oldest Uploads'
  • View the latest videos uploaded by all the users
  • Infinite scrolling

Tech Stack

Client: Angular, TailwindCSS

Server: Firebase
